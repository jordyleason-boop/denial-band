About Denial

Welcome to the world of Denial, a four‑piece alternative punk band from the heart of the North East of England. We are the “Noisy Geordie Bastards” bringing raw energy, heavy riffs, and unapologetic attitude to every stage we step on.

Who We Are

Denial is built on a shared love of punk rock and the wider world of music. Each member brings their own influences, creating a gritty, powerful sound driven by pounding drums, fierce vocals, and a rebellious spirit that captures the essence of the Geordie attitude.

Our Journey

Formed in and around the North East, we’ve been making noise across the local scene with electrifying live shows and hard‑hitting tracks. Every member adds their own flair, shaping a dynamic and explosive sound that stands out from the crowd.

Our Music

Our songs reflect real experiences — frustrations, social issues, personal struggles, and the fight for individuality. We aim to connect with listeners on a deeper level, giving a voice to anyone who feels unheard.

Join the Revolution

We’re not just a band; we’re a movement. Come to a gig, feel the energy, and become part of the Denial family. Together, we’ll make some noise and show the world what it means to be true “Noisy Geordie Bastards”.
